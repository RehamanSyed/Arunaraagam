G. ARUNA

B High grade

M.A. Music, A.U. Visakhapatanam.

Guru : Isai Peroli Dr. Smt Pantula Rama
Parents : Sri M.Venkat Rao & Tulasi

Father being Musician Started Learing Music from 4 Years of Age. Performed at so many Prestigious Organisations and Sabhas all over India.

Prizes

  • Secured many 1st Prizes in Several Competitions Conducted at various places like Visakhapatnam, Vijayawada, Bilaspur, Hyderabad, Gwalior, Kakinada, Chidambaram and many more.

  • Secured 2nd Prize in the National Level Youth Festivals Conducted at Jiwaji University, M.P. in 2004

Awards

  • Kurella Sita Mahalakshmi Award for upcoming artist in the Year 2002

  • Best Contest Award by Visakha Music & Dance Academy in 2002.

Experience  

  • Taught Music for 45 days under the Programme called “KALAPARICHAYAM” conducted by government.

  • Worked as Music Lecturer in RGUKT - IIIT NUZVID For 3 Years

  • At Present PRINCIPAL OF “ VIDYADHARI SCHOOL OF MUSIC ” KAKINADA

Achievements

  • B-High grade in All India Radio, Visakhapatnam in 2010 and Many More awards in State & District Level Competitions.

Online Training Classes

I am a carnatic Classical Vocal Performer cum Music Teacher with more than 20 years exposure in performing and teaching a number of students.

Approved All India Radio Artist (B-High) and given performances in all over India and other states in temples, religious activities and prestigeous sahbas and organisations.

Being Principal of "Vidyadhari School of Music", I have been training students from fundamentals to advanced course and sending them to Diploma, Certificate, M.A Course and also sending them to live performances at several places etc., and also taking online classes trough Skype and Zoom.

Initial training under my father Sri.M.Venkat Rao who is a well known Carnatic Vocalist and Composer in light, classical and devotional.

After that I am Blessed to be the Disciple of "Isai Peroli" Dr. Smt Pantula Rama, a National Wide leading Carnatic Vocalist.

Looking forward for more opportunites to spread my music all over the world with blessing of my Gurus.
